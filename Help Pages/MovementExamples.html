Movement

There are two methods of moving robots: by coordinates, or like a turtle. With the coordinate method you specify the coordinates and heading of a robot and it can be very useful. The turtle method makes the robot's movements dependant on the robot's pose (position and orientation).

While for the coordinate method to draw a square you must loop over 4 points, the turtle method simply loops over 2

It can also be helpful to mix ans match methods. I started the robot at its corect initial position, but if I wasn't sure where the robot was from some previous turtle steps, I could use the move to point block. That initialy positions the robot for its square driving
